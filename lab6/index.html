25 Years of
Scientific Discovery

November marks 25 years of human presence aboard the International Space Station. Since the first crew arrived on Nov. 2, 2000, NASA and its partners have conducted thousands of research investigations to advance exploration of the Moon and Mars and benefit life on Earth.
